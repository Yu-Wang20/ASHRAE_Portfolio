Statistical Inference Report

A rigorous walk through classical OLS diagnostics on the ASHRAE energy dataset. Every Gauss-Markov assumption is tested and quantified — multicollinearity (VIF), heteroscedasticity (Breusch-Pagan), non-normality (Shapiro-Wilk), autocorrelation (Durbin-Watson), and influential points (Cook's Distance). Each violation is used as mathematical justification to advance the modelling approach.
